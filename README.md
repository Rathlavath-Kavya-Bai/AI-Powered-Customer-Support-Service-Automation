# AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation

## Overview

The **AI-Powered Customer Support & Service Automation** system is designed to streamline customer service operations using **Salesforce Service Cloud**, **Flow Builder**, **Prompt Builder**, and **Agentforce (AI Service Agent)**.

The system helps an international Electronics & Gadgets company efficiently manage customer inquiries related to product issues, order tracking, warranty claims, and general support. By automating repetitive tasks and leveraging AI-driven responses, the solution improves response times, enhances customer satisfaction, and provides better visibility into support performance.

---

## Objectives

- Automate customer support processes.
- Improve response time using AI-powered service agents.
- Centralize customer, product, and case management.
- Reduce manual effort through workflow automation.
- Generate reports for monitoring support performance.

## Project Modules

### Customer Management
- Store and manage customer information.

### Product Management
- Maintain product details and warranty information.

### Order Management
- Track customer orders and delivery status.

### Case Management
- Create, assign, and resolve customer support cases.

### Queue Management
- Automatically route cases to the appropriate support teams.

### Approval Process
- Automate warranty approvals and escalations.

### AI Service Agent
- Answer customer queries using Agentforce.

### Reporting & Dashboard
- Analyze case resolution performance and customer support metrics.
